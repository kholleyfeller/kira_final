                              Venice Beach

You haven't seen it all until you've seen Venice! There is a sandy three-mile beach here, but that is not what attracts visitors. Venice is a residential, commercial, and recreational beachfront neighborhood of the city of Los Angeles in the Westside region of Los Angeles County. The Boardwalk is a virtual sidewalk circus, a walk 'n' rolling skin show.

    Santa Monica Beach

Originally built in 1909, the Santa Monica Pier is home to an amusement park and an aquarium, as well as dining options ranging from gourmet fare to cotton candy and ice cream cones, and much more. With the beach breeze in the background you have endless options at this location.
